Happy Birthday

ادین
Eternal Paradise

To Aden, whose name means Eternal Paradise...

May your life be as calm as moonlit waters,

As endless as the stars above,

And as beautiful as gold dancing in the dark.

Happy Birthday, ادین — You are paradise in motion.

Dear Aden (ادین),

On this special day, may the moonlight guide your path, and may your heart be as bright as the stars above.

Happy Birthday! 🎂✨
